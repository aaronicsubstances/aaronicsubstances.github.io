Psalm 99 (100 in Tanakh)
Praise God for His Goodness: Serve the Lord with Gladness
Enter with Exultation and Song, Praise and Thanksgiving
1  (A Psalm for a Thank-offering)
Shout for joy to the Lord, all the earth.
2  Serve the Lord with gladness;
enter His presence with exultation.
3  Know that the Lord Himself is our God;
it is He who made us, and not we ourselves;
we are His people and sheep of His pasture.
4  Enter His gates with thanksgiving
and His courts with songs;
give thanks to Him and praise His name.
5  For the Lord is good, His mercy is eternal;
and His truth continues from generation to generation.
