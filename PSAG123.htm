Psalm 123 (124 in Tanakh)
A Pilgrim Song of Thanksgiving to the Saviour
Our Help is in the Name of the Lord
1  (A Song of Ascents)
‘Unless the Lord had been among us,’
(let Israel now say),
2  ‘unless the Lord had been among us
when men rose up against us,
3  they would have swallowed us alive,
when their fury blazed against us.
4  The water would have drowned us,
our soul would have passed through a torrent.
5  Our soul would have passed through
irresistible water.’
6  Blessed be the Lord,
Who did not give us as a prey to their teeth!
7  Our life, like a bird, has been saved
from the snare of the hunters;
the snare has been broken and we have been saved.
8  Our help is in the name of the Lord;
Who made heaven and earth.
