Psalm 119 (120 in Tanakh)
A Pilgrim Song: The Enemies of Peace
The Arrows of Love
1  (A Song of Ascents)
To the Lord in the hour of my distress
I cry and He answers me.
2  O Lord, deliver my soul from lying lips,
and from a treacherous tongue.
3  What should be given you,
what added protection,
against a treacherous tongue?
4  The sharp arrows of the mighty warrior
with coals of desolation.†Cp. Psalm 139:11; Rom. 12:20. ‘Coals of hell’. For desolation as the equivalent of hell, see Mark 13:14.
5  Woe is me, that my pilgrimage is prolonged,
that I live among the tents of Kedar.
6  My soul has long been on pilgrimage
with those who hate peace.
7  I am peaceful:
but when I speak to them,
they fight me without provocation.
