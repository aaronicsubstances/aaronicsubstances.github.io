Psalm 149
Triumphal Song of the Redeemed
Weapons of World Conquest: Prayer and Praise
1  (Alleluia)
O sing to the Lord a new song;
sing His praise in the Church of the saints.
2  Let Israel rejoice in Him Who made him,
and let the children of Zion rejoice in their King.
3  Let them praise His Name with a dance,
and sing praises to Him with tambour and harp,
4  For the Lord takes pleasure in His people,
and will exalt the humble with salvation.
5  The saints will exult in glory,
and will rejoice on their beds.
6  Let the high praises of God be in their mouths,
and a two-edged sword in their hands;†two-edged sword: praise which conquers Amalek (St. Chrysostom).
7  to pass judgment on the nations,
and give rebukes to the peoples;
8  to bind their kings with chains,
and their nobles with fetters of iron:
9  to pass judgment on them as God has written.
This glory is for all His saints.‡‘Holiness of life is the mother of glory’ (St Theodoret).
