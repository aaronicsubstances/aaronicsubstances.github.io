Psalm 128 (129 in Tanakh)
A Pilgrim Song: The Gates of Hell will not Prevail
We Bless you in the Name of the Lord
1  (A Song of Ascents)
‘Often have they warred against me from my youth,’
(let Israel now say),
2  ‘Often have they warred against me from my youth;
yet they could not prevail over me.
3  The sinners plowed on my back;
they prolonged their iniquity.
4  But the just Lord
breaks the sinners’ necks.’
5  Let all who hate Zion
be confounded and routed.
6  Let them be like grass on the housetops
that withers before it is pulled up,
7  with which no reaper can fill his arms,
no one gathering sheaves fill his bosom.
8  And no passers-by will say to them:
‘The blessing of the Lord be upon you;
we bless you in the name of the Lord.’
