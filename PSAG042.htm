Psalm 42 (43 in Tanakh)
Light and Truth from God’s Holy Mountain
The Way to God is the Way of the Altar
(A Psalm by David)
1  Judge me, O God, and defend my cause
from an unholy nation;
deliver me from the wicked and treacherous man.
2  For thou, O God, art my strength.
Why hast Thou rejected me?
And why must I go mourning
with my enemy oppressing me?
3  O send out Thy light and Thy truth
that they may lead me and bring me
to Thy holy mountain and to Thy dwelling.
4  And I will go to the altar of God,
to God, the joy of my youth.†The way to God is the way of the altar of the cross (Heb. 4:16; 13:10). God is the joy of the new man born crucified. Joy renews life. (St Athanasius)
I will praise and thank Thee
on the harp, O God, my God.
5  Why are you downcast, O my soul?
And why are you disquieting me?
6  Trust in God, for I will praise and thank Him;
He is the salvation of my person and my God.
