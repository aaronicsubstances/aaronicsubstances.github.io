Psalm 116 (117 in Tanakh)
World-Wide Call to Praise the Lord
Mercy and Truth Meet without Merging
1  (Alleluia)
All you nations, praise the Lord!
Praise Him, all you peoples!†Rom. 15:11.
2  For strong is His love for us,
and the Lord stays true to His word forever.
