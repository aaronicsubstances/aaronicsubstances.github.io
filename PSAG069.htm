Psalm 69 (70 in Tanakh)
The Lord be Magnified, my Helper and Redeemer
Let All Who Seek Thee Rejoice in Thee
1  (By David, In remembrance that the Lord has saved me)
2  O God, come to my help;
O Lord, make haste to help me.
3  Let all who seek my life†Ps. 69:3-7 differs only slightly from Ps. 39:15-18.
be put to shame and confounded.
Let all who wish me evil
be turned back and put to shame.
4  Let those who say of me, ‘Fine, fine!’
be turned back immediately ashamed.
5  Let all who seek Thee
rejoice and be glad in Thee, O God;
and let those who love Thy salvation
say continually, ‘The Lord be magnified.’
6  But I am poor and needy;
help me, O God.
7  Thou art my helper and my redeemer;
O Lord, make no delay.
