Psalm 46 (47 in Tanakh)
The Great King of the Earth: His Triumphant Ascension
A Call to All Nations to clap for Joy: Love Reigns
1  (A Psalm for the sons of Korah)
2  All you nations, clap your hands;†‘The calling of the Gentiles is to be understood, as in 71:9’ (St. Athanasius).
shout to God with cries of joy.
3  For the Lord Most High is terrible;‡Cp. Heb. 12:28,29; Deut. 4:24; 9:3; 10:17-21; Neh. 1:5; Zeph. 2:11.
He is the great King over all the earth.
4  He subdues peoples under us,
and nations under our feet.
5  He has chosen for us an inheritance for Him,§Cp. Ex. 15:17; Is. 58:14: I Pet. 1:4.
the beauty of Jacob whom He loves.
(Pause)
6  God has gone up with a shout,
the Lord with the sound of a horn.
7  Sing praises, sing praises to our God;
Sing praises, sing praises to our King.
8  For God is King of all the earth;
sing praises with understanding.
9  God is reigning over the nations;
God is seated on His holy throne.
10  Rulers of peoples gather together
with the people of the God of Abraham,
for God’s strong men of the earth
are highly exalted.†‘Who are God’s strong men? They are the Apostles and all the faithful. They are strong because they faced the whole world, they overcame all, and nothing could defeat them’ (St. Chrysostom).
