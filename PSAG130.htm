Psalm 130 (131 in Tanakh)
A Pilgrim Song: The Spirit of Humility and Childhood
Weaning is Growth in Freedom and Strength
1  (A Song of Ascents. By David)
O Lord, my heart is not haughty,
nor my eyes lofty;
I do not meddle with great things,
nor with marvels beyond me.
2  If I am not humble-minded,
but lift up my neck,†Prov. 29:1; 2 Kings 17:14; Neh. 9:16; Dt. 9:6.
like a weaned child against its mother,
Thou wilt pay my soul back.
3  Let Israel trust in the Lord,
from now and for ever.
