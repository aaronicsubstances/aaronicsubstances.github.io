Psalm 133 (134 in Tanakh)
A Pilgrim Song of the Night
Intercessors Standing in the Holy Spirit
1  (A Song of Ascents)
Behold now bless the Lord,
all you slaves of the Lord,
who stand in the house of the Lord,
in the courts of the house of our God.
2  At night lift up your hands to the Holy of Holies,†A suppressed tōn hagiōn is to be understood here (cp. Heb. 8:2; 9:3,8,12,24,25, wrongly translated ‘holy place’ in R.V. etc.) In the liturgy of St Basil and in the church services God is addressed as the ‘Holy of Holies.’ (1 Tim. 2:8).
and bless the Lord.
3  May the Lord Who made heaven and earth
bless you out of Zion.
