Psalm 8
The Greatness of God and His Love for Men
The Greatness of Man as God’s Plenipotentiary
1  (A Psalm by David)
2  O Lord, our Lord,
how wonderful is Thy name in all the earth!†‘By His name death is destroyed, demons are bound, heaven is revealed, the gates of paradise are opened, the Spirit is bestowed’ (St Chrysostom).
For Thy majesty is exalted above the heavens.
3  Out of the mouth of babes and sucklings
Thou hast perfected praise‡Mt. 21:16.
because of Thy enemies,
to silence the enemy and the avenger.
4  When I behold the heavens, the work of Thy fingers,
the moon and stars which Thou hast poised,§poised: lit. fixed.
5  what is man that Thou rememberest him,
or a son of man that Thou visitest him?
6  Thou hast made him a little lower than the angels;
Thou hast crowned him with glory and honor,
7  Thou hast set him over the works of Thy hands;
Thou hast put all things under his feet:†Heb. 2:6-8. The Sovereignty of the Son of Man (Messiah).
8  all sheep and cattle,
and also the beasts of the plain;
9  the birds in the sky and the fish in the sea,
and the things that pass through the paths of the seas.
10  O Lord, our Lord,
how wonderful is Thy name in all the earth!
